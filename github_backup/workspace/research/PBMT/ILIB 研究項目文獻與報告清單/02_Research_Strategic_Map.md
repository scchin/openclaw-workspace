# Research Strategic Map: Intravascular Laser Irradiation of Blood (ILIB)

## 1. Project Overview
**Objective:** To transition ILIB from a fragmented collection of clinical observations to a scientifically validated, standardized, and regulatory-compliant medical intervention. The goal is to create an executable proposal that defines the "Minimum Viable Protocol" for therapeutic efficacy while navigating the complex ethical landscape of blood manipulation.

## 2. Research Axes & Expert Deployment

### Axis I: Molecular & Cellular Bio-Mechanisms
*   **Specialized Expert:** `Molecular Photobiology Agent`
*   **Key Themes:** Wavelength-Specific Signaling, Heme-Dynamics, Systemic Modulation, Mitochondrial Activation.
*   **Goal:** A "Biological Mechanism Matrix".

### Axis II: Clinical Efficacy & Protocol Optimization
*   **Specialized Expert:** `Clinical Research Architect Agent`
*   **Key Themes:** Indication Prioritization, Dosing Standardization, Outcome Metrics, Comparative Analysis.
*   **Goal:** A "Standardized Clinical Protocol Handbook".

### Axis III: Technical Implementation & Hardware Engineering
*   **Specialized Expert:** `Biomedical Laser Engineer Agent`
*   **Key Themes:** Delivery Modalities, Power Calibration, Device Stability, Safety Engineering.
*   **Goal:** A "Technical Specification Document".

### Axis IV: Regulatory, Ethics, & Compliance
*   **Specialized Expert:** `Bioethics & Regulatory Consultant Agent`
*   **Key Themes:** Anti-Doping Compliance (WADA M1.3), Medical Certification (FDA/EMA), Ethical Framework, Legal Liability.
*   **Goal:** A "Compliance & Ethics Roadmap".
