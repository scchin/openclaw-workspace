# Standardized Clinical Protocol Handbook: ILIB

## 1. Indication Prioritization
| Indication | Evidence | Metric | Efficacy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Metabolic Syndrome | High | HbA1c, Glucose | High |
| Cardiovascular | Med-High | BP, RBC Deformability | High |
| Chronic Pain | Medium | VAS, IL-6 | Med-High |
| Insomnia | Med-Low | PSQI, Cortisol | Medium |

## 2. Clinical Protocols
### Protocol A: Metabolic Syndrome
*   **Wavelength**: 810nm (Pref) or 660nm.
*   **Dose**: 30-45 min/session, 3x/week, 4-8 weeks.
### Protocol B: Cardiovascular Health
*   **Wavelength**: 810nm.
*   **Dose**: 20-30 min/session, Daily (5x/week), 2-4 weeks.
### Protocol C: Chronic Pain
*   **Wavelength**: 660nm.
*   **Dose**: 30-60 min/session, 3-5x/week, 3-6 weeks.
### Protocol D: Insomnia
*   **Wavelength**: 660nm or 810nm.
*   **Dose**: 30 min/session, 2-3x/week, 4 weeks.

## 3. Outcome Metrics
*   **Objective**: HbA1c, Lipid profile, hs-CRP, $\text{IL-6}$, $\text{TNF-}\alpha$, BP.
*   **Subjective**: VAS (Pain), PSQI (Sleep), FSS (Fatigue).
