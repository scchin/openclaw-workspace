# Biological Mechanism Matrix Report: Intravascular Laser Irradiation of Blood (ILIB)

## 1. Molecular Analysis
### A. Wavelength-Specific Signaling
*   **Red (632.8–660 nm) & NIR (808 nm)**: Targets CCO $\rightarrow$ Displaces NO $\rightarrow$ Increases ATP (30-40%).
*   **Blue (405–440 nm)**: Targets Porphyrins $\rightarrow$ ROS modulation $\rightarrow$ Immunomodulation.
*   **Green (520–532 nm)**: Targets Hemoglobin $\rightarrow$ Platelet membrane stabilization $\rightarrow$ $\downarrow$ aggregation.

### B. Heme-Dynamics
*   **RBC Deformability**: Enhances membrane fluidity $\rightarrow$ better microcirculatory perfusion.
*   **Platelet Reactivity**: Inhibits hyper-reactivity $\rightarrow$ lowers micro-thrombosis risk.

### C. Systemic Modulation
*   **NO Release**: $\text{NO} \rightarrow \text{sGC} \rightarrow \text{cGMP}$ $\rightarrow$ Potent systemic vasodilation.
*   **Cytokine Inhibition**: Downregulates $\text{TNF-}\alpha$, $\text{IL-6}$, $\text{IL-1}\beta$ via $\text{NF-}\kappa\text{B}$ modulation.
